To answer this question, let's analyze the pros and cons between buying a new and second-hand vehicle.

New Cars for Sale

Pros
  • Full warranty package
  • New features and technology
  • Various options to choose
  • Longer service life
  • After-sales service
Cons
  • Expensive price (including tax)
  • Higher monthly installments
  • Devaluation (when you want to sell)

Used car for sale

Common myths are that used cars are of poor quality and unreliable, which is not true. It is just for saving money that people buy used cars. Used cars are of good quality and are fully maintained to give their long service.

Pros
  • Affordable price
  • Quicker payoff
  • High-quality models
  • Lower loan amount
Cons
  • Limited choice
  • Higher rate
  • No warranty (when purchasing with a private party)
  • Low reliability
  • No after-sales service
